why choose us?

We give you the best quality: We offer only the finest quality at various price points. We do not sell throw away items! There is no one else in town that carries the brands we do or is as committed to service as we are.

We save you time: Are you limited on time? We will keep your sizes and tailoring instructions on file, allowing you to rapidly come in and make selections or order items over the phone, text or email.

We save you money: We will always make sure you are aware of sales on the brands you love! We also have many different brands to choose from ensuring that you are paying the price that makes sense for you.

We make sure you look your best: We will be meticulous in ensuring you have the best fit possible, whether it be off the rack or custom. Tailoring is included with everything purchased here, so whether it is a jean that needs hemming, or a coat waist taken in, we will make sure it is done right.

We give you the best service: We are not your typical department store. We take great pride in what we do and we are here to help you every step of the way. We are passionate about taking care of our customers and have many long-standing relationships with clients due to this approach. Once we get to know you we will truly personalize your shopping experience with us.

We troubleshoot outfit problems for you: We will build entire outfits, wardrobes, seasonal collections, etc… you can be involved as much or as little as you want in this process. It truly is our pleasure to make sure you look and feel great!

We will not pressure you: We are not on commission and do not want to sell anything to you that you don’t truly want or need. We are here to help you find what works best for you, whether it be a pair of socks or a custom suit. We are committed to having a working relationship with each of our clients. We will be here to help you with your first suit all the way to your retirement party dinner jacket.

We will make sure you are effective: If your line of work requires business dress, we will make sure you have the garments you need to be maximally effective at your profession. We believe that how one dresses matters and impacts their effectiveness. If you are serious about your business, we are your store.
